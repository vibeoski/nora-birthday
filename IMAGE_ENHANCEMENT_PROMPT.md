## Image Dimensions & Ratios:

**Milestone Images:**
- Recommended: 400x300 pixels (4:3 aspect ratio)
- Alternative: 800x600, 1200x900, or 1600x1200 (maintains 4:3)
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Max file size: 500KB (web-optimized)

**Hero Image (Main Photo):**
- Recommended: 600x600 pixels (1:1 square ratio)
- Alternative: 1200x1200 or 1800x1800 (maintains 1:1)
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Max file size: 1MB (web-optimized)

**Lightbox Display:**
- Images will scale to fit screen (max 90vh height)
- Maintains original aspect ratio
- Works best with 4:3 or 3:2 ratios for milestone photos

**Best Practices:**
- Use 4:3 ratio for milestone timeline photos (portrait or landscape orientation)
- Use 1:1 (square) ratio for hero/main photo
- Ensure images are well-lit and centered
- Leave some breathing room around the subject (don't crop too tight)
